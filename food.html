10 foods that are good for health


1. Lemon

One lemon turned out to already meet 100 percent of the daily requirements of vitamin C, which can help increase good cholesterol levels and strengthen bones. Flavonoids antioxidants that exist in Lemon can also help inhibit the growth of cancer cells and act as an anti-inflammatory.

2. Broccoli

One broccoli sprig contains more than 100 percent of the daily requirement of vitamin K and nearly 200 percent of the recommended vitamin C. With these two nutrients not only have the function of strengthening bones but also have the same portion to prevent various types of cancer.

3. Dark Chocolate

Not only delicious, but useful. By consuming a quarter of an ounce of chocolate every day can reduce blood pressure in healthy people. Chocolate also has a lot of flavonoids, anti-oxidants which are proven to reduce bad cholesterol.

4. Salmon

A useful source of Omega-3 fatty acids reduces the risk of depression, heart disease, and cancer. With three ounces of salmon, then you already meet 50 percent of daily niacin needs that are useful for preventing memory loss and Alzheimer's.

5. Avocado

Avocados have healthy fat content that is proven through research can reduce cholesterol by 22 percent. And with just one Avocado, you already get more than half the fiber and 40 percent of folate needed per day, which can help reduce the risk of heart disease.

6. Garlic

Have complete nutrition to fight all types of diseases, because it can inhibit the growth of bacteria including Eschericia coli. The content of allicin, works potently anti-inflammatory and is proven to reduce cholesterol and blood pressure.

7. Nuts

Nuts One serving of nuts (peanuts, peas and lentils) consumed four times a week can reduce the risk of heart disease by 22 percent, including reducing the risk of breast cancer

8. Tomatoes

Tomatoes are vegetables as well as fruits which are part of almost all culinary needs. Tomatoes help reduce the risk of several dangerous diseases, death rates and obesity. The health benefits of tomatoes include improving eyesight, good stomach health and reducing blood pressure. Antioxidants and vitamin C in tomatoes help fight free radicals against cancer. The high content of lycopene has a role in preventing cancer. In addition, tomatoes contain choline, potassium, fiber and vitamin C which help improve heart function. In fact, this concurrently vegetable fruit is also good for facial skin health.

9. Spinach

Spinach contains lutein and zeaxanthin, two antioxidants that are important for maintaining eye health. Because according to new research, compared to other fruits and vegetables, spinach is one of the most effective.

10. Oatmeal

Oatmeal is often referred to as a superfood because it has many benefits for health. Oatmeal is also a food with a high source of carbohydrates and fiber. So no wonder, oats or wheat are often used as a substitute for rice specifically for those who are on a diet. A bowl of oat-meal in the morning can reduce the risk of high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, and lower cholesterol. The high fiber content makes oat-meal an ideal breakfast menu. You can add fresh low-sugar fruit, nuts, or calcium-rich milk as a topping.
